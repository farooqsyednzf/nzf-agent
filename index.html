What is Zakat?

Islam has five core pillars: fundamental practices that every Muslim is called to observe. Zakat is the third. It's an annual obligation to give a fixed portion of your wealth (2.5%) to those who genuinely need it.

It's not charity in the optional sense. For Muslims who own wealth above a minimum threshold (called the Nisab), Zakat is a religious duty. It's designed to circulate wealth through society and ensure no one in the community is left behind.

Who we help

Real people. Real need.

The Quran identifies eight categories of people who are eligible to receive Zakat. NZF focuses on helping Muslims in Australia who fall into these categories. Many of them you'd never know were struggling.

🏠

Families in Crisis

Facing eviction, unable to pay rent or utilities, or fleeing unsafe situations.

👩‍👧

Single Parents

Often isolated, with no second income and children depending on them.

🏥

Illness & Disability

Unable to work due to medical conditions, surgery recovery, or disability.

🌏

Refugees & New Arrivals

Rebuilding from nothing, often with no government support or right to work.

📖

New Muslims

Converts who've lost family support after embracing Islam.

🌿

The Working Poor

Employed but still unable to cover rent, food, or school fees as costs rise.

About us

What makes NZF different?

NZF was founded in Australia in 2013 with one focus: to manage Zakat properly, locally, and with full accountability to Islamic principles.

Most people pay their Zakat overseas, to international charities working in conflict zones. NZF asks a different question: what about the Muslims right next door who are quietly struggling?

We're a registered charity with full DGR (tax deductible) status, governed by an Islamic framework, and transparent about every dollar we receive and distribute.

  • ✓ 100% Zakat compliant. Distribution follows Quranic principles, not just general charity rules.
  • ✓ Locally focused. Every dollar given to NZF stays in Australia and helps Australians.
  • ✓ Casework, not handouts. We assess each application carefully to ensure funds reach those with genuine need.
  • ✓ Tax deductible. All donations above $2 are fully tax deductible in Australia.
  • ✓ Trusted by scholars. Our approach is grounded in Islamic scholarship, not just administration.
